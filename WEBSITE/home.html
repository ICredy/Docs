Our Services

Decentralized Identity Solutions

Our decentralized identity solutions revolutionize the way entities issue credentials securely and efficiently. By establishing a credential schema and credential definition, entities lay a strong foundation for issuing verifiable credentials. These credentials are anchored on the Hyperledger blockchain, ensuring immutability and integrity, eliminating the need for centralized intermediaries. Individuals gain true control over their digital identities, empowering them with privacy and sovereignty over their personal information. With our decentralized identity solutions, users can confidently share their credentials in various contexts like job applications and financial transactions, knowing that their data is protected against unauthorized access and tampering.

Credential Storage in Wallets

At ICredy, we empower users with secure and user-friendly digital wallets to manage and store their credentials efficiently. These wallets enable easy access to verifiable credentials while ensuring the privacy and control of personal information. Our cutting-edge encryption techniques guarantee that sensitive data remains protected. Whether it's showcasing qualifications during employment opportunities or verifying identities in financial interactions, our wallet systems provide a seamless experience. With ICredy's wallet solutions, individuals can present their credentials confidently, eliminating the need for relying on central authorities and reducing the risk of identity theft or data breaches.

Credential Verification Services

Trust and verify effortlessly with ICredy's blockchain-based credential verification services. Businesses and organizations can rely on our robust processes to verify the authenticity of presented credentials. Leveraging the transparency and immutability of the Hyperledger blockchain, our verification services ensure only valid and tamper-proof credentials are accepted. This fosters a highly secure environment, safeguarding against fraud and enhancing trust in the decentralized identity ecosystem. By streamlining verification processes, ICredy enables quicker and more reliable identity checks, making it an indispensable tool for businesses and institutions seeking to validate their users' identities accurately.

Interoperable Solutions

ICredy believes in fostering a collaborative and inclusive ecosystem by designing our decentralized identity solutions with interoperability in mind. Our services seamlessly integrate with existing systems and other blockchain networks, allowing users to enjoy true ownership of their identities across different platforms and applications. This interoperable approach breaks down barriers and enables a smoother user experience. Users can efficiently share their verifiable credentials across various services and ecosystems, enhancing the overall adoption and utility of decentralized identity solutions. Through our commitment to interoperability, ICredy plays a vital role in driving the widespread adoption of decentralized IDs and promoting a more connected digital world.
